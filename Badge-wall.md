# 🏅 ZK Contributor Badge Wall

This badge wall showcases **verifiable, governance‑aligned contributor reputation** for this repository.  
Badges are issued by the **GitDigital Badge Authority** based on **zk‑verified contributions** and **governance actions**.

---

## 🎯 Badge Tiers

### 🟢 Starter Tier

- **Badge:** `ZK-Contributor: Seed`
- **Who:** New contributors with their **first zk‑verified contribution**
- **Examples:**
  - 1 merged PR
  - 1 accepted issue
  - 1 governance‑aligned task completed

---

### 🔵 Core Tier

- **Badge:** `ZK-Contributor: Core`
- **Who:** Consistent contributors with **proven impact**
- **Examples:**
  - 5+ merged PRs
  - 10+ accepted issues
  - Participation in governance discussions
  - Passing zk‑proofs for contribution quality

---

### 🟣 Steward Tier

- **Badge:** `ZK-Contributor: Steward`
- **Who:** Contributors who **shape direction and governance**
- **Examples:**
  - Leading features or modules
  - Reviewing and approving PRs
  - Proposing and implementing governance changes
  - Maintaining high trust score over time

---

### 🟡 Guardian Tier

- **Badge:** `ZK-Contributor: Guardian`
- **Who:** Long‑term, high‑trust contributors with **ecosystem‑level responsibility**
- **Examples:**
  - Multi‑repo stewardship
  - Security and protocol reviews
  - Governance proposal authorship
  - Cross‑ecosystem integrations

---

## 🧩 Badge Metadata

Each badge is:

- **zk‑verified:** Backed by zero‑knowledge proofs of contribution
- **Governance‑aligned:** Issued according to `governance.json` and `badge-rules.json`
- **Portable:** Can be referenced across GitDigital zk ecosystem apps
- **Audit‑ready:** Every issuance is logged and verifiable

---

## 🤝 How to Earn Badges

1. **Contribute:** Open issues, PRs, or governance proposals.
2. **Align:** Follow the repo’s governance rules and contribution standards.
3. **Verify:** Your actions are processed by the ZK‑Contributor‑Reputation‑App.
4. **Receive:** When thresholds are met, a badge is issued by the Badge Authority.

---

## 🌐 Ecosystem Context

Badges from this repo can be:

- Aggregated into **cross‑repo contributor profiles**
- Used in **governance weight calculations**
- Referenced in **grant, bounty, or rewards programs**
- Displayed in **public contributor dashboards**
